Selena Quintanilla

Queen of Tejano Music

“I don’t feel pressure because I’m not trying to portray something that I’m not.” – Selena Quintanilla

Summary

My tribute is on the late Selena Quintanilla. She was born 1971 Lake Jackson, Texas; Selena was very much admired and for her musical and fasion designed talent, her flamboyent and sexy charisma, her selflessness and kind heart. She started performing in a family band with her brothers before her solo performances and few believed she would have success as young woman in the Tejano genre.

    Fun Facts

  • English is Selena's first language she sang spanish; teaching herself spanish and speaking it fluently later on in her life.
  • When Selena's family noticed her musical talent they did everything they would to cultivate and develop them.
  • At the age of 10 years old she became the lead singer of her family's band Selena Y Los Dinos.
  • Selena would designed and create the outfits she would wear onstage for her preformances.
  • Selena's first album with a major compnay came out in 1989.
  • Selena started secretly dating her husband Chris Pérez when he joined her band in 1990.
  • At the 1987 Tejano Music Awards, she won both "Best Female Vocalist of the Year" and "Performer of the Year."
  • In 1993 Selena won a grammy for Best Mexican-American Album for her album Live!
  • On March 31, 1995 Selena was murdered by the leader of her fan club/manager of her boutique.
  • Selena named the greatest female latin artist of all time by the Billboard.
